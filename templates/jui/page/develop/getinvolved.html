Adopt Agile Toolkit in your company

We love open-source and we love PHP. Unfortunately apart from some software applications, PHP is often not regarded in enterprise as a serious language. We intend to change that by offering a solid platform focused on business needs yet exceedingly efficient, fast and secure.

If your company produces Web software, consider using the Agile Toolkit. If your business environment has some requirements which Agile Toolkit can't meet, please let us know

Promoting Affero GPL Software

Affero GPL v3 is a license which makes sure that any software built on it remains open-source. Unlike other web frameworks which allow closed-source development, we would like more and more software to be built on ATK4 as open-source software. released. If your company is build some internal software, and you would not loose anything if was open-source, consider licensing your project with AGPL. Even with commmercial software, it may make sense to release an open-source version as part of your product strategy.

Add-on Developers

Agile Toolkit makes it extremely easy to develop add-ons. Practically anything you develop can easy be distributed as an add-on. See our add-on development section and learn how easy it is to share parts of your project with community.
