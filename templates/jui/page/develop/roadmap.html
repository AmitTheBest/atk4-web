Community Site

We are integrating authentication with 3rd party OAuth providers to make it easy for you to register with agiletoolkit.org. Once you are registered, you will be able to to much more on this site. We also plan to launch some satellite sites:

  • Forum - simple to use forum / discussion board. Like it? Download and use it yourself.
  • Messaging system - send other members private messages
  • Profiles - build your profile by showing what you have done with Agile Toolkit. List yourself as freelancer, company or consultant, etc
  • Project repository - what projects based on Agile Toolkit are out there? Let's list open-source projects and put more information about them

Agile Builder

Agile Builder is an initiative to create WYSIWYG builder for your web application. You would be able to go online, and start putting together your application through a very visual process. Once you are done, you will be able to get the source code and copy into your page for further modification

  • Model builder - add fields to define your business models. Preview how forms and grid will look with your model
  • Interface builder - add elements and mash them together. Join them with models you have created
  • Form builder - beat other commercial form-builders at their own game
  • Easy export. Download your own application with a click of a button.
