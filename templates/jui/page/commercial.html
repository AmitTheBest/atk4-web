How much faster is to develop with Agile Toolkit?

A project which would typically take 3 months to be developed from scratch on other PHP framework can be built in a single month with Agile Toolkit. When compared to development with no framework at all, the speed up might be up to 10 times.

If you are building a seriously large project, then benefits of Agile Toolkit will prevail even when your product is in production. Adding new functionality or changing existing functionality is much easier and simpler with Agile Toolkit.
