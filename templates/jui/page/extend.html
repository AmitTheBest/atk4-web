Toolkit Add-ons

Agile Toolkit takes a great pride in how extensible it is. There are many powerful add-ons for the Agile Toolkt that you can use in your own commercial or open-source projects. You can also contribute add-ons to the Agile Toolkit community and if they meet coding standards, we will publish them on our website

Good Quality Add-ons

"When I use some other frameworks my frustration was that I had to develop everything myself. There were some add-ons but they were of a poor quality and they didn't fit into my application at all. Authors had different licenses and were often lacking updates, which caused a lot of trouble for our commercial projects. And when we tried to modify them ourselves, we discovered a lot of spaghetti code." Romans Malinovskis, CTO Agile Technologies

Through compliance with Agile Toolkit Coding Standards you will make it very easy to integrate third party Add-ons or t create your own. The Agile Toolkit approach, ideals and techniques are a little different from other libraries and frameworks. Once you are familiar with those, you will find working with Agile Toolkit faster, simpler, and more addictive. Our Interactive Introduction will go through concepts step-by-step and introduce you with the basics of Agile Toolkit.

For those willing to start from the core of the the toolkit, we suggest that you start with the API Reference or look at Agile Toolkit Sources on GitHub.com

Learning from Examples

Conventions is what makes Agile Toolkit simple. Once you can see through the conventions, even features you never used before will be familiar to you from the first glance. Our Example section is created to serve two purposes: to show the framework features and to demonstrate how to solve the daily problems web developers encounter.

If screen casts is your thing, there are several available: Getting started in 5 minutes; Starting a new project from Zero; Working with Web UI and AJAX

If you are willing to look at what real project looks like, there are several you can examine: Colubris - The Agile Team Tracker; SalesJotter - Lightweight CRM for you; or you can even take the source of www.atk4.com website: Website Source - www.atk4.com;

The Community

Our belief is that the new generation of Web Software relies in the hands of our community. We are here to give you the core foundation of ATK and make it available for both open-source and commercial projects. We also have provided some of the add-ons. What remains for you to do is to think of a great idea and get it working within weeks. Many have already did so, so come and share experience, suggestions and tips inside our forums.

Agile Toolkit is popular with SMEs, Startups and other companies. We want all of you to be successful and invite different companies to collaborate and integrate their on-line software. By using same platform it's much easier. If you are building or planning to build a website using Agile Toolkit - let us know and we will be able to offer you a lot of competitive benefits
