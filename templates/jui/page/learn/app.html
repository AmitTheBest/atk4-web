Practical Guide to your First Application

Congratulations. If you have following this book up to this point, you should have a solid understanding about Agile Toolkit and how it works. It is time now to get practical and start working with your own application.

In this guide we will be creating a DVD rental application. This application would be handy for on-line booking of DVD titles. It will consist of two interfaces, one for the customers, another for the operator.

Please follow the guide on your local copy of Agile Toolkit. You can also look at a full code at github.com/atk4/dvd-rental.

Agile Development

World of software said it - Agile Software Development is the way to go. We will be taking quite open-minded approach for application building and initial requirements. Let's make it fun.
