Models

Models is yet another gem of Agile Toolkit. Implementation of Models in Agile Toolkit is lightweight, very intuitive and elegant way to define your business logic.

If you have used ORMs before, you would probably find Agile Toolkit Models more self-sufficient, often incorporating some of the controller logic and designed to keep objects in your application securely separated and organised.

Use of Models in Agile Toolkit is optional, but very highly recommended for anything more sophisticated than a contact form.
