Learning should be fun!

While you are trying to learn Agile Toolkit, let's keep it fun and less formal. But first - the good news:

Examples are as easy as they look

Have you seen those blogs which teach you to do things? You follow the advise and create 5 files do multiple tweaks and then finally you can see some result?

Examples in Agile Toolkit documentation are different. They are short and compact. Except Agile Toolkit you don't need to have antyhing. If you have gone through installation documentation, you would know where to put your example code.

Do not hestitate to copy-paste any example into your local installation of Agile Toolkit. You'll find out that button shape, layout or font-size may differ to match configuration of your environment, but the result and logic remains the same
