Documentation

The Agile Toolkit approach, ideals and techniques are different from other libraries and frameworks. Once you are familiar with those, you will find working with Agile Toolkit faster, simpler, and more addictive. Our Interactive Introduction will go through concepts step-by-step and introduce you with the basics of Agile Toolkit.

For those willing to start from the core of the the toolkit, we offer to read through our API Reference or look at Agile Toolkit Sources on GitHub.com

Learning from Examples

Conventions - it's what makes Agile Toolkit simple. Once you can understand the conventions, even the features you were never used before will become familiar to you from the first glance. Our Example section is created to serve two purposes: to show the Agile Toolkit's features and to demonstrate how to solve daily problems web developers encounter.

If screen casts is your thing, there are several available: Getting started in 5 minutes; Starting a new project from Zero; Working with Web UI and AJAX

If you are willing to look at what real project looks like, there are several you can examine: Colubris - The Agile Team Tracker; SalesJotter - Lightweight CRM for you; or you can even take the source of www.atk4.com website: Website Source - www.atk4.com;

The Community

Our belief is that the next generation of Web software wil be developed through commercial and open-source communities like ours. We are here to give you the core foundation of Agile Toolkit and make it available for both open-source and commercial projects. We also have provided some of the addons. What remains for you to do is to think of a great idea and have it working within weeks. Many have already did so, so come and share experience, suggestions and tips with us.

Agile Toolkit is popular with SMEs, Startups and other companies. We want all of you to be successful and invite different companies to collaborate and integrate their on-line software. By using same platform it's much easier. If you are building or planning to build a website using Agile Toolkit - let us know and we will be able to offer you a lot of competitive benefits.
