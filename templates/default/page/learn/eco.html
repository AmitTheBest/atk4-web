Agile Toolkit not just out there as a semi-maintained piece of software under MIT license without warranty of any kind.

Our dual licensing scheme sends a clear message. We care about future of Agile Toolkit. We have spend great effort making framework simple, complete, full-featured and documented. We will continue to pay a lot of effort on it and we pay our core developers to ensure best quality. Agile Toolkit is precious to us and that's why we are not ready to hand it away just to anyone.

When you use Agile Toolkit, you become part of it's eco-system. You can use Agile Toolkit for free but you would need to help us make it better.

Our goal is to make Web development simpler. Agile Toolkit is only a foundation. Without your support and your contribution we can't do it.
